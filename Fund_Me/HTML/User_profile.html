I love Cornell and am so happy to go here! The faculty, students, and campus are incredible and I am so lucky to bleed Big Red. My dream is to start my own ed-tech company one day and I believe with the right attitute and a Cornell education my dream will come true. I plan on taking a few machine learning and data science courses as an upper-classman to take my CS skills to the next level.

Message to Donors

To My Generous Donors:

When I got into Cornell the first thing I thought was "I cannot believe it". The second thing I thought was "How in the world am I going to pay for this?" My family insisted that education is paramount and attend my favorite university and worry about the money later. I worked hard to find ways to fund my education through private scholarships but the chances of winning with so many candidates was slim and it was hard to find a scholarship that fit me. That meant I was going to have to take out student loans to help fund my tuition.

I cannot tell you how much it means to me that a group of alumni would empathize with an aspiring female computer scientist enough to donate back. This will only help my dreams of running my own company closer to a reality as I now have to worry less about my tuition payments than I have before.

To all of you, THANK YOU!
